50-minute train disruption between Boon Lay and Joo Koon

03 May 2016

A train fault caused a 50-minute disruption on the East-West Line between Boon Lay and Joo Koon, with no train service in both directions.

A train fault on the East-West Line (EWL) caused a 50-minute disruption between Boon Lay and Joo Koon stations, reported The Straits Times. 

For about 50 minutes, there was no train service on the EWL between Boon Lay and Joo Koon stations in both directions. Transport operator SMRT tweeted at about 3:45pm, "Due to a train fault, there is no train service between JooKoon and BoonLay in both bounds. Expected resumption time will be 4:05pm." 

Train service between Joo Koon and Boon Lay stations resumed at 4:30pm, after further clarification from SMRT. Free regular bus service was provided during the disruption.
