NEL service resumes after signalling fault at Sengkang station


08 Sep 2015


The North-East Line service has resumed after a delay due to a signalling fault at Sengkang station on Tuesday (Sept 8th) morning.

The North-East Line (NEL) service has resumed after a delay due to a signalling fault at Sengkang station on this morning, reported The Straits Times.

According to SBS Transit's Twitter account, at 7:54am, NEL service has resumed. However, free bus rides are still available between Punggol and Farrer Park stations.

Commuters were earlier told to expect an additional travelling time of 10 minutes.
