No train service between Yishun and Yio Chu Kang: SMRT

11 May 2016

There is no train service between Yishun and Yio Chu Kang MRT stations due to a train fault, SMRT reported at 4:01pm.

There is no train service between Yishun and Yio Chu Kang MRT stations due to a train fault, SMRT said on Wednesday (May 11th), reported The Straits Times. 

Service is expected to resume at 4:25pm, the operator said in a tweet at 4:01pm.

The disruption affects three stations on the North-South Line - Yishun, Khatib and Yio Chu Kang.
