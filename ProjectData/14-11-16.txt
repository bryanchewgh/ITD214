Train service disrupts along North-South Line from Yew Tee to Jurong

14 November 2016



According to tweets and Facebook posts, there is a train disruption along the North-South Line on Monday morning. Based on what have been posted, the disruption seems to have started since late 6am and still on-going.

The fault according to a tweet, shows the disruption between Yew Tee and Jurong East. However, this means those after Yew Tee, such as Woodlands and Yishun who are travelling to Jurong East MRT interchange would be affected as well.
